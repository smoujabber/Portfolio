MITXPRO Project descriptions

  • The Pacmen exercise was made in week 7 of the MIT Full Stack Developer Program. The goal of this project was to create a game where the pacmen would bounce off the screen and move in random directions.
  • The Moving Eyes exercise was made in week 8 of the MIT Full Stack Developer Program. The goal of this project was to get the pupils to follow/track the movement of your mouse.
  • The Real Time Bus Tracking exercise was made in week 9 of the MIT Full Stack Developer Program. The goal of this project was to track the bus stops between MIT and Harvard. Mapbox and MBTA API were used in this project.
